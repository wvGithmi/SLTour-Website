Wildlife Tours

Sri Lanka is one of the best countries that have nice bio-diversity in the world. You can see more wild animals including the world's largest land mammal, elephant. Sri Lanka is home to the largest seasonally recurring concentration of wild elephants found anywhere in the world. There are so many thrilling wildlife safaris you could choose from Sri Lanka. You'll be able to take lots of photographs, as well as experience our unique ever-smiling mix of people and cultures. You'll simply have a great time here! Sri Lanka has 26 Wildlife National Parks that are popular and frequently visited.

Yala National Park
Wilpattu National Park
Minneriya National Park
Udawalawe National Park
Wasgamuwa National Park

Beach Fun

Sri Lanka is a beautiful island located in the Indian ocean. Although we have numerous Golden beaches that are fringed with coconut palms making it the ideal destination for a beach holiday. One of the best parts of Sri Lankan tours is these wonderful sun-kissed sandy stretches. According to the country's coastal regions, you can choose what most suits your choice. A large percentage of luxurious resorts and villas, as well as budget hotels, are located along the coastal areas to offer tourists some of the best beach holidays in Sri Lanka. You can taste fresh seafood also.

Mirissa Beach
Beruwala Beach
Hiriketiya Beach
Passikudah Bay
Arugam Bay Beach

Heritage Sites

Sri Lanka has nearly 3000 years of rich and great history with ancient cities, palaces, temples, monasteries, hospitals, cafes, etc. UNESCO has also listed 8 World Heritage sites in Sri Lanka. These places are located in a historical location with cultural and anthropological importance. These are all very popular attractions among locals and foreigners alike. You can know the historical stories behind these ancient places and feel the beautiful Sri Lankan culture. You can plan your trip on your choice.

Sacred City of Kandy
Ancient City of Sigiriya
Golden Temple of Dambulla
Sacred City of Polonnaruwa
Sacred City of Anuradhapura
Old Town of Galle and its Fortifications

Diving Tours

The island of Sri Lanka is a paradise for diving lovers. There are many exceptional wrecks, dazzling coral reefs and, of course, an extremely rich marine life and flora. Mostly the waters of Sri Lanka are home to an incredible range of marine creatures. The great Diving spots in here make for a fun experience whether you are well-experienced in diving or are an amateur. The warm nutrient-rich waters, the hot tropical weather, and an ideal location make Sri Lanka the perfect holiday destination for lovers of diving. Around the country, there are many diving centers for beginners also.

Panadura Wreck
Goda Gala Diyamba
Hikkaduwa Diving Tour
Kalpitiya Bar Reef Diving Tour
Trincomalee and Nilaveli Pigeon Island Diving Tour

Tea & Culture

Formerly known as Ceylon, Sri Lanka stands as the world’s fourth-largest producer of tea. The tea-drinking culture within this island country is strong, with Sri Lankans drinking on average three cups per day. Sri Lanka has become the producer of some of the world’s finest teas, from single origins to mixed fruit blends. Ceylon tea is one of Sri Lanka’s primary exports. It's not a complete trip without spending little time in these beautiful tea estates and feeling the climate of Hill Country of Sri Lanka. There are many tea plantations and estates that you can visit here.

Dickoya Estate
Pedro Tea Factory
Haputale Lipton's Seat
Virgin White Tea Plantation
Uva Halpewatte Tea Factory
