Tour Guides

Tourist guides provide the visitor with in-depth knowledge in their mother tongue, smoothing creases and wrinkles that are bound to occur while touring a foreign country for the first time.
Sri Lanka Tour Guides are trained and certified by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority (SLTDA). Those who are trained for touring throughout Sri Lanka are called National Tour Guides or Chauffer Tourist Guides whereas guides who are based on the location of any of the tourist attractions are called On-Site Guides. It is best to choose a tour guide who had been professionally trained and authorized by Sri Lanka tourism.
The National Tourist Guides caters to a large group of tourists traveling in luxury coaches driven by reserved drivers while Chauffeur Tourist Guides would be guiding small groups conveyed in small vans and luxury cars.
